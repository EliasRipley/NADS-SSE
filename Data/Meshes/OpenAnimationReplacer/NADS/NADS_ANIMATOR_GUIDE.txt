NADS Animator Guide (OAR Templates)

NADS ships OAR submod templates but no bundled HKX dodge clips.
Default out-of-box behavior is native controller-driven movement plus vanilla invisibility FX.

Direction code mapping (iNADSDodgeDirection):
1 = Forward
2 = Right
3 = Backward
4 = Left
5 = RightForward
6 = RightBackward
7 = LeftBackward
8 = LeftForward

Recommended shout-slot filenames to override per template folder:
- MT_Shout_Exhale.hkx
- MT_Shout_Exhale_Medium.hkx
- MT_Shout_Exhale_Long.hkx
- 1HM_Shout_Exhale.HKX
- 1HM_Shout_Exhale_Medium.hkx
- 1HM_Shout_Exhale_Long.hkx
- Sneak1HM_Shout_Exhale.hkx
- Sneak1HM_Shout_Exhale_Medium.hkx
- Sneak1HM_Shout_Exhale_Long.hkx

Authoring rule:
- Avoid MCO_/DMCO_ annotation events and root-motion displacement in bundled clips.
